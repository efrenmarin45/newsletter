Creating the ultimate clean, luxurious lip balm was always something of a holy grail for us here at goop—and it took us three full years to do it. “To say we had to kiss some frogs is an understatement,” GP laughs. “It had to be super cushy, moisturizing, and nourishing. It had to feel amazing and perform even better. It had to have the creamiest slip, a smooth finish, a subtle scent, and just a whisper of a taste.”


Because we consume much of what we put on our lips, the highest-quality clean ingredients were more important than ever. “My lips get so dry in the winter especially,” says GP. “This balm soothes and moisturizes like nothing else.” The satin finish leaves a hint of gleam: “You notice this subtle glowy something when you smile,” says GP. “Most days, this lip balm is the only makeup I wear. And, no small thing: best gift ever.”

“Something really great is worth searching for, dreaming of, waiting for, and even insisting upon,” says GP.
